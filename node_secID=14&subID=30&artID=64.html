farastu

Submitted by ripvanwormer on Sat, 2011-08-27 19:10

Farastu are a type of Gehreleth.

Farastu, or tarry demodands, are the lowliest of their kind. They continuously secrete black tar from their pores that clings to their foes and weapons. They are tall and slender.

Farastu are forced to do the most menial tasks in demodand society, under orders from kelubar and shator superiors. When around weaker creatures they are great bullies. On other planes they may set themselves up as petty lords or kings.

Sources: 
Planescape Monstrous Compendium Appendix I
Fiend Folio (3ed)
Monstrous Compendium Outer Planes Appendix
Monster Manual II (1st edition)

Zadara

Submitted by ripvanwormer on Mon, 2011-08-08 09:57

Zadara is a Titan who lives in Sigil, one of the Golden+Lords with unfathomable wealth (and she's a member of the Merkhant sect). She fled to Sigil from the world of Rublia, where she had set herself up as a god until a real god, the greater power Skeartim, chased her off the planet, intending to kill her. Zadara has remained in Sigil ever since.

Sources: 
Uncaged: Faces of Sigil
