Stone Gullet of L'Phahl the Gross

Submitted by Carnifex on Thu, 2004-09-09 01:00
in
  • Artifact
  • Equipment

This unusual item came from an attempt to play a joke on a sorcerer's apprentice called L'Phahl, a well known glutton. At a meal, his wine goblet was filled with a potion of flesh to stone. However, the gastronomic young man consumed so much food and drink that the potion was diluted, and it was not until several hours later when he complained of a stomach ache that it was discovered that his stomach had turned to stone, though it was still capable of digesting food. After his death, many years later, his stomach was removed as an oddity.

Ravel's Fingernail

Submitted by Carnifex on Thu, 2004-09-09 01:00
in
  • Artifact
  • Equipment

This twisted fingernail of extruded night hag skin has got to be one of the ugliest things you have ever seen – it came from the legendary night hag Ravel Puzzlewell, who was killed in her maze by a powerful being called the Nameless One. Its mottled green, red, and black surface is almost a foot long, and it seems like it was used alternately as a scratching aid, nose-picking tool, and gardening trowel. Looking at it makes you wish that Ravel did chew her nails. Or at least had a nail file with her in the maze.
