Ring of Watery Miracles

Submitted by Anonymous on Sat, 2004-11-20 02:00
in
  • Magical Item
  • Equipment

These rings were "gifts" from a group of Lower Planar fiends. They operate normally as rings of water walking, but they only work for one hour at a time, and have ten charges per week. However, while this power is in effect, any cleric who can see the wearer must make a Will save (DC 15) or feel an uncontrollable desire to follow the wearer of the ring onto the water. The ring's power does not extend to these unfortunates, which was probably the intent of the fiends.

Gloves of the Manipulator

Submitted by Anonymous on Sat, 2004-11-20 02:00
in
  • Magical Item
  • Equipment

This pair of gloves has a texture similar to that of smooth leather, and looks much like any other pair of fashionable gloves. However, in fact they have been made from the skin of a planar being that relies on trickery and deceit for its living, such as an arcanoloth or glabrezu, and spells have been bound into them to make the wearer himself better able to beguile and deceive others. This gives any wearer a + 10 circumstance bonus to both Bluff and Diplomacy skill checks.

Sensate Amulet

Submitted by Anonymous on Sat, 2004-11-20 02:00
in
  • Magical Item
  • Equipment

This amulet originates in the city of Sigil, where it was forged by the Society of Sensation. Amulets such as this are given out to the most loyal Sensate factotums to protect them when they travel the planes looking for new experiences. While worn, it grants a +4 enhancement bonus to charisma, as well as 5 bonus hit points. Furthermore, it grants continual Protection from Evil.
