Cascade

Submitted by Anonymous on Tue, 2004-11-23 02:00
in
  • Psionic Power
  • Equipment

Cascade allows a psion to place a number of powers "on hold" within a valuable gem. At a later date, the psion can release "held" powers, as a free action, at a rate of up to 2 per round.To instill powers within the gem, the psion must spend one hour per level of the power for each power placed in the gem, and spend the necessary 17 power points to manifest cascade. At this time, the psion must spend the normal cost for manifesting the powers (i.e., 13 Power Points for a 7th Level Power).

Tongue Charm

Submitted by Anonymous on Sat, 2004-11-20 02:00
in
  • Magical Item
  • Equipment

This charm is a small, shrivelled tongue, obviously obtained from some humanoid creature. Its roughly textured surface varies in colour dramatically, from a rusty flesh colour through to a bluish white. Green and other exotically toned tongue charms are often used, clearly signalling the wide variety of creatures from which tongues are obtained to be prepared for the magical process. Across the dried surface of the tongue, barely visible runes can be distinguished, having either been painted onto it or stitched into the charm.
