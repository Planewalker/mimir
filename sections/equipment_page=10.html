Charcoal Charm

Submitted by Anonymous on Tue, 2004-11-23 02:00
in
  • Magical Item
  • Equipment

This piece of charcoal is a charred bone fragment of some creature, perhaps a finger bone or a talon. Various symbols have been scratched onto its surface... the scratchings are so faint that they are easy to miss. This charcoal charm temporarily protects the user against flames and extreme heat. To use the charm, the charred bone is snapped and the broken ends rubbed over the heart of the user. The effects are that it absorbs up to a total of 60 points of fire damage.

Yevrah's Ring of Almost Invisibility ( Type II )

Submitted by Anonymous on Tue, 2004-11-23 02:00
in
  • Magical Item
  • Equipment

This is Yevrah's Ring of Almost Invisibility. It makes its wearer invisible - almost. Sometimes, for brief periods, the wearer becomes really, *really* close to being invisible - an event which is often disconcerting to an attacker - and is thus more difficult to strike in combat.Wearing the ring grants +1 enchantment bonus to wearer's AC, as well as allowing him to cast spell Blur 3 times per day as a spellcaster equal to his level. Finally, the wearer gets a circumstance bonus of +10 to Hide checks.

Elemental Staff of Power

Submitted by Anonymous on Tue, 2004-11-23 02:00
in
  • Magical Item
  • Equipment

An Elemental Staff of Power is a very powerful magic item, heavily imbued with the energies of the Elemental Planes. It appears as a slender staff inlaid down one side with clear crystal, one side with fire opal, one side with lapis lazuli and one side with polished granite; it is topped with a large diamond within which encaged elemental forces can be seen swirling and mixing. This type of Staff of Power is highly prized by any practitioner of magic due to the control over elemental forces that it gives them.
