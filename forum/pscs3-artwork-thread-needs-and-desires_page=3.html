I'm with Duster on the blue filter - I think it darkened it a bit much. What really stands out to me is the sky - it's very flat with no texture whereas the rest of the piece has the grainyness of pencil on paper. So it stands out as something intserted in post-scan. What you may want to do is experiement with ways to match the textures of the sky to the rest of the piece. I think that will help it merge better with the rest.

The only other thing I would note is that with that heavy dark sky, and some of the other coloration, it seems as if the Ditch itself (i assume that's the Ditch?) is under bright light, and the background under dark. I think that's the effect you were trying to fix with the blue filter. What you may want to do is deepen the shadows in the Ditch so that the depth of shadows in both areas match. That make sense? If you apply the blue filter in Just the background / far end of the Ditch that may also give that feeling of depth and shadowy background you were looking for.

Believers of The Source Factioneer

Qiignawg is actually a prime dwarf who reincarnated as an elf after one of his mishaps at the core of a volcano which was a vortex or sumsuch. He changed his life views with his newfound chaotic nature, but kept his mining fervor as he's mining far reaches of multiverse. He's mining for his soul.

Ring-Givers Factioneer

Ts'colo Fawl is an ancient thiefling with some goblin blood in his gut and he is one of the most devious loan sharks to prowl the Hive & Lower Ward.His luck has grown since the new tax laws in Sigil have been set. Since there aren't any Fated left, it is not only his job to take back what he's given(with interests), but his way of life.

This is an older picture and I've added a faction symbol to the top of his hat(it's also drawn by Squaff).

Athar Factioneers

Bern, Goghar (God Dog, his artist name) and Edmundy (goblin beatbox) are the musical bard crew from the Lower Ward. Bern is a drummer & has a really deep voice for his mocking rhymes. Edmundy is here only for booze & women. This unique crew is currently trying to minimize the number of temples and churches by giving them bad publicity with their clever rhymes.

Also an older picture but I've added Athar symbols to their loincloths. This way they look even more "bling bling" since Squff drawn the symbol with a metallic quality and I've adjusted the color for each brother(they aren't real brothers).
