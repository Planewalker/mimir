i wonder how far we can 'teleport', thus if we are close by enough, and we knew where to port to, right?

800 feet (400' + 40' per level, with ECL 10 characters)

800 ft is pretty far in an urban environment, so the greenskirts on the corner are well in range of Ii. You can D-door anywhere you can visualize within this range. You can also D-door in any direction to a place you can't see ( i.e. "300 feet thataway, 50 feet down" ). If the space is occupied by matter alrady, you spend a round 'inbetween' before popping out in the closest available space.

Ii already used 1 of 3 D-doors for today. Racial abilities work like thus: You'll get the 3 per day back when you get a full rest ( 8 hours of sleep )

To Dire and/or any other lurkers, potential players, former and current players of BoG'r:

I've said so before, but anyway ...

This game is rated "DND Mature" as defined in various 3.x publications. I don't want to break Planewalker's "Grandma Rule", (i.e. don't post anything that will offend your gandma), but then again, my Gram watches hockey. Hockey.

Combat will be graphic and bloody. Mature themes will be explored, etc. This is the theme of the campaign. I'm going for a kind of horror/adventure feel.

That said, I've never asked anyone's ages, real names, home addresses, etc. If someone wishes such things be known they wil volunteer said information. The game is strictly "read at your own risk" and, by extension "play at your own risk". I expect, in the spirit of community, and in the name of good taste, someone will let me know if and when I take it too far toward the "vile content" (as I had assumed Zim did when she quit right before the assault on the Hive Brothel)
