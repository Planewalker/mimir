EDIT

Big idea! This adventure may, toward the end, embroil you in the politics of the multi-planar empire of the githyanki, and how it sustains itself on the former Incursion worlds. I used FR for my Incursion and this adventure because it is well detailed and everyone has a map (and many of you sourcebooks which I DON'T).

But anyway, after this adventure chapter, you could eventually go to an original-design world. One of DM, or even PLAYER design.

Bigger background for BoG'r:

There were nine true Incursions, along with a few smaller raids and diversions. Only a very few githyanki would have known of them all, and even the greatest generals only might have known of two or three beyond his own. The true reason for the Incursion is also not common knowledge among githyanki, though the PCs are all privy to it.

PC Knowledge:

Ya'shenn served as a pilot/scout/coordinator/interrogator on Savim, a world where the graith races were being conquored by illithid and by various other aberation races. Many graith nations were ruled by demonic cults or oppressive monarchies puppeted by illithid. Thus the general populous began to greet the githyanki as their saviors. The githyanki were far from saviors, but they shrewdly used the tide of uprising, and even worked well on occasion together with the barbarians.

Ii'jyka'vaar served here on Toril, but left very early on.

I forgot (if Az told me ) where Ar'dru served.

The PCs know that Gith's Sword was found, and that it the apparent cause of the return of Gith as Ar-Gish. *Certain* PCs know the Lich Queen was also looking for Gith's Dagger and Gith's Tiara. DM Note: "Tiara" is a cool word, and I can invision several forms of jewelry which could conceivably become known as a "Tiara" in lore.
