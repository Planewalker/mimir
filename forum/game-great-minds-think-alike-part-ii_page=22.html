The group enters the palace, and walks up the stairs to the highest tower. A few yards up the stairs, they find the bloody, flayed corpse of a fiend - a rutterkin, to those who know these low-ranking tanar'ri. A close inspection of the dead body, if anyone cares to search, reveals nothing extraordinary.

A bit up the stairs, an open door leads into a large wooden room, which seems to have burned long ago - the darkened wood on the walls and some piles of ash is all there is that could be called "interior". But here, again, the group finds a corpse - this time the body of a vrock, ripped apart. The fiend seems to have died the same way as the rutterkin, probably in a bloody battle against some other monster.

Finally, the group reaches the highest point of the stairs, leading to a square door three yards high, and made entirely of marble. A single wooden ring set into the door's exact center is the only way to open it.
