Hey there

Heya, folks. I've decided to introduce myself after a few months of kinda popping in and out at random times to take a look at things. My name's Alex and I am a massive fan of Planescape. I think its the best-made setting out there in its stunning detail and unique flavor. I've made a few minor contributions here and there, such as an extremely biased and abrasive letter to the editor in an issue of the Lady's Sharper Eye as a tout named Zath.

All I can really say is 'wow', this is a great community and it warms my heart to see some die-hard Planescape fans carrying on the legacy.

I'm hoping to take a more active role in things now and contribute here and there if its alright. I adore the setting and have even made a habit of dragging it into things like the Neverwinter Nights server I DM on. Unfortunately, I've moved a few times in the past and have lost touch with all of my PnP friends, so I can't run any tabletop Planescape games.

At any rate, I see a possible page of mindless babble from yours truly on the way, so I'm going to wrap it up before to comes to that.

Good to meet you all and I hope to see you around.

-MaO
