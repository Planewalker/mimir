I've got a bit of time in the coming week and beyond, so I'd like to start a game based on a premise of mine from awhile ago.

Four mortals are trapped on Carceri, waking up on the banks of the Styx with no memory of who they are or what they are doing there... escape is the primary goal.

If this sounds up your alley, "Flotsam" needs four 5th level equivalency (3.5 rules) player characters. Any race with a 4 or less LA is good. (So you can be a 4th level Aasimon Fighter, or a 3rd level Centaur Druid, or a 2nd level True WereBoar Barbarian... Or another thing - your call.

No established characters, please. And don't worry about possessions - I'll take care of that - it's part of the story.

Just respond or PM me if you're interested, and we'll start as soon as I have my four.

Hence why I only wanted you to send me stats. Your characters remember NOTHING, so why do anything for a write-up? Just put this in my hands, for now, and let the story write the characters.

You remember nothing. Name, faction affiliation, alignment, deities worshipped, history... NOTHING. The styx erases all.

Insinct, however, remains. Your physical prowess remains. You may still be intelligent, wise, or charismatic - these things do not change. And who knows? You may even regain these memories and knowledge beyond.

Hence the questions of this campaign.

This is not a traditional campaign. There will be much character and player freedom to rediscover their character, and Carceri will shape you as much as you shape Carceri with your beliefs.
