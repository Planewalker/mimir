Re: Planescape: Metamorphosis - A Planescape Webcomic

Thank you! I am always open to more involved comments I don't know how many readers outside of Planewalker are actually Planescape fans, so it's hard to get a sense of what planars think sometimes. I know things have been kind of slow and I'd love to be able to update 3 or more days a week, but I simply don't have that kind of time.

Now that I've been updating regularly for over a month, here are a few stats for that month that I've pulled off of Google Analytics for anyone that's interested. I really like seeing these little tidbits because it's something I frequently wonder about whenever I visit a website.

The past month I have had:

Visitor Statistics
- 1,851 visits from 967 unique visitors for a total of 7,438 page views. That makes an average of 4.02 page views per visitor. Each visitor spends an average of 1 minute, 45 seconds on the site.
- Average 59.71 visits per day. The day with the most visits over the past 30 days was yesterday, at 100 visits. The day with the least was Sunday, August 23, with 33 visits. Over the past month, I have had a 45% increase in new visitors.
- Unfortunately, my Bounce Rate is 63.48%. I'm not really sure how to reduce that without moving the latest page off the index. That's something I don't want to do because I think potential readers should see the latest page immediately.

Visitor Locations
- By far, most of my visitors (1,047 out of 1,851) come from the United States. Canada is second place with 149 visitors and Sweden is third with 90. The remaining places on the top 10 with the most visitors are in order: Germany (78), United Kingdom (62), Australia (61), Poland (55), France (44), Italy (37), and Russia (31).
- I have gotten visitors from all over the world, including a few from Turkey (12), Colombia (9), the Philippines (3), and Israel (2)

Browsers
The following browsers are most used when visiting my comic:
- Firefox (62.56%)
- Internet Explorer (18.21%)
- Safari (6.75%)
- Google Chrome (6.59%)
- Opera (4.65%)

Visitor Retention/Loyalty
- 845 visitors visited the site one time last month. However, I have a fair amount of loyalty since 166 visitors visited 9-14 times, 199 visitors visited 15-25 times, and 154 visitors visited 26-50 times.
- Most (66.56%) visitors stay on the site for 10 seconds or less and most (63.48%) never look past the front page.

Where visitors come from
I'm not sure if this is entirely accurate since "planescapemetamorphosis.com" is a parked domain that runs over my regular domain. If you go to www.kaitoukage.net you also get the comic site. Google Analytics is told to look at kaitoukage.net, so I have a *lot* of referrals directly from planescapemetamorphosis.com and they may be skewing the numbers. If someone more familiar with domain parking and Analytics could help me figure out the numbers, I'd be very appreciative. I haven't used Analytics much before this comic because I haven't had a "real" website up. Anyway, here are some numbers for your enjoyment.

- Most visitors find out about the comic from Planewalker. I have gotten 450 visits from the links posted here during the last 30 days. Thank you all very much for the support since I started!
- Myth Weavers is second behind "planescapemetamorphosis.com" referrals. 98 visitors over the past month have come from Myth Weavers. Not bad considering I have only recently started advertising there.
- Twitter advertising comes next with 82 visitors. I think this number might be falsely reported because of the referrals (I have 445 visits from direct links and 445 more from planescapemetamorphosis.com referrals and I'm pretty sure more people come from Twitter)
- LiveJournal brings in a fair number of visitors, though I can't tell from this chart if it's people looking at my site from their friends pages or if I'm being linked. The numbers are not all lumped together, but taken as a whole, I probably have 80-100 visitors from LiveJournal sites. I can see that Bob the Efreet is among those who have visited from LiveJournal, so thanks, Bob! I think I may start crossposting updates to the Planescape community and a couple D&D communities.
- Facebook and deviantART bring in a small amount of visitors. Somewhere along the line I got added to the D&D wiki at dnd.wikia.com and I've received 8 visitors from that link. I wonder if the wikipedia entry for Planescape has me listed? If not, maybe I should add myself. There's another comic that calls itself Planescape (though after a glance through it I haven't seen very much that's Planescape about it), so who knows. Fortunately, a Bing search for "planescape webcomic" has me listed as the top three hits

Thank you all for your support so far. After really digging through my Analytics report, I know that the fans and readers are out there, even if you're quiet sometimes. I really appreciate you guys reading and standing behind me with this effort and I will do my best to keep this going and not let you down.
