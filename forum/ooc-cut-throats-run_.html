Hello all. I know, I tried to start a campaign about a year or so ago, and, to no avail, thanks to myself. I was young and pig-headed. I've grown up a little bit, so, I know a little more. I've had a little more online play experience and DMing experience. Now to get to the point:

I want to try to start a new online game. I wont be able to start it off until sometime after Christmas, but, by then, hopefully everything will be squared away here at home. Setting will be FR, but, watery. The basis for the game is a contest being held in Waterdeep among the lowest of the low. Yes, I'm speaking of Fearun's dark under belly, the criminal syndicates of the realms. Players should be roughly seventh level. When you're making your character, think criminal. No alignment restrictions or Organization restrictions. It'll be 3.5, and any classes or prestige classes need to be ran by me first. I have an array of books, so, most will be open.
So, any takers?
Reply here if you are interested.
