All the fancy gold and crystal fixtures have been long looted, leaving narry a doorknob behind. There are some concessions to luxury, however, reflecting the current use of the place. There are several greenskirts without the status to rate a semi-private room lounging on matresses in the wide hallway, and Daraz can see a pair of hobgoblin eunuchs standing guard at the top of the stairs at the other end.

The Hive Brothel

A satyr, immaculately groomed, heavilly pierced, and reeking of vanilla, approaches Ya'shenn and Daraz. He offers his services as a 'guide'.

Ya'shenn feigns concern, "Sir, when we came through our portal, it looked like one of your customers was killing one of your greenskirts and robbing her. He was heavily armed ..."

"Wh ... What!?! Why that cross-trader! Guards!" He calls to the pair of burly hobgoblins watching the stairs.

"Now's our chance," she whispers to Daraz. The short commotion also attracts the attention of the various greenskirts, and only one notices Ya'shenn and Daraz go down the stairs. Yashenn notices her, a youngish human with short blonde hair and makeup concieling a bruised cheek. "Come with us." Ya'shenn tells her, and she obeys. As they descend the stairs, Ya'shenn speaks quickly to the greenskirt, acertaining her disposition toward her 'employers'. The prostitute answers truthfully and easilly, as if chatting with an old friend. She is (unsurprisingly) unhappy with her life, being little more than a pleasure-slave to the lowest scum of the planes. She harbors resentment and a stubborn spite, but she is also kept in line through fear. Ya'shenn stops her on the stairs, and they lock eyes. "You hate your masters. As if watching a dream, you will not react to violence against them. You will lie, or go along with the lies of others, to bring them subtle harm."

Daraz, decending the stairs a few steps ahead of Ya'shenn and the greenskirt, sees a pair of hobgoblin guards below. The staircase is a sweeping marble spiral, with a space in the middle. There is no railing, for it must have been tore out and looted some time ago. The guards do not notice Daraz. They are armed with stout clubs, and one has a whip coiled around his torso. Both also have daggers in belts at their waists. They are otherwise unarmored and unclothed, and both are eunuchs.

From the knowledge that Ya'shenn had taken from Grazz's mind, the gith know that before them lie a number of old, but well made, cellars and vaults that the illithid have stocked with their own mixture of oppulent and horrific luxuries. Once past the guards that Daraz now sees, there is a long decending ramp, likely guarded at the far end as well. Beyond that they should be able to sneak around the cellars, hopefully avoiding detection, for most of the rooms will be empty. At one end of the 'inner offices' is a hallway with several vaults with the doors removed (all but one, which is the Hive Brothel's treasury). These are the portals to other rooms in Illithid House. Once there, Daraz and Ya'shenn are to get ready to ambush any illithids fleeing the warriors' attack.

The Airspace Above the Hive Ward

The sulfurous clouds over the Hive, are as bad, in their own way, as the fumes of Avernus. Ar'dru's draconic lungs can tolerate such punishment, but the smell is still nausiating. Then there are the unpredictable air currents, which seem to get far more unpredictable over the Hive Ward. Though he is not the only flier in the Sigilan 'sky' Ardru's most dangerous opponent remains Sigil's 'weather'. Ar'dru, heavilly laden with flasks and ceramic jars, does not have an easy time of it, and is even forced to land near the Gatehouse for a few minutes. Once he takes wing again, he soon catches sight of the Marble District. After a few careful passes, he identifies the half-ruined mansion housing the Hive Brothel.

The Hive Brothel - Exterior

The brothel is housed in a sprawling, partially ruined marble mansion. There are three different wings, one more ruined and squalid than the others. The main house has three levels, the lowest one tall enough for a titan. The archetecture is awe-inspiring, but it has been a long time since the Marble Disctrict was taken over by the Hive. The white marble exterior is stained with long red-brown streaks of oxidation from Sigil's rain. Doors, railings, and slabs of marble have been carted away and sold by hivers. In the more-ruined wing, whole sections have been replaced by shanties of scavenged blocks, wood, and tarps. Even in the two better-kept wings, windows and doors are made of rags.

On the main mansion, there are a few balconies, some empty, some with carousing patrons and greenskirts. All are oblivious to Ar'dru's presence.

The Hive Brothel

(upstairs)

Ar'dru begins soaking the cheap furnishing with oil. The greenskirt, too startled at first to summon an adequate response to the intrusion, backs away toward the door, lacing up her bodice as she goes.

"Hey smeg! If you're into redskirts, they've got 'em downstairs. I'm not up for anything kinky involving fire though, so you can count me out." She sneers at Ar'dru as she prys up a loose floorboard to retrieve her stash of jinx.

[Yea, I had to make her smarmy, sorry. Just remember, you're evil and allowed to kill her.]

(downstairs)

The dominated human greenskirt needs to be led, like a sleepwalker, until the hobgoblin corpses are out of sight. Afterwards, she snaps out of her stupor and begins chatting like nothing happened. Through her, Daraz and Ya'shenn learn that the guards will not listen to her, a mere greenskirt, but will listen to one of the 'associates'.

"We may use this to get the drop on the next pair of guards we see. It is good to change tactics periodicly."

The greenskirt does not know the layout of the lower chambers herself, few who are sent there ever return. Neither does she know the race or identity of the masters. She, like many of her fellows, think that either the pimps make up "The Masters" or that they are an ever-changing group of the richest, most powerful pimp/slavers in The Hive.
