Ar’dru and the

Ar’dru and the abomination begin to slowly circle one another, each carefully assessing the others form for any chink in the defence. The Yuan-Ti attacks first, feinting a bite before lashing out with its wickedly curved blade, Ar’dru avoids the worst of the strike, twisting his body around in a quick spin to take himself off the line of the attack, but he still takes a deep gash across his left arm. In spite of this, the manoeuvre builds enough torque to deliver a mighty blow to the serpents side, carving a thin fillet out of the giant serpent.

The combatants quickly return to their circling, fencing now and then to test each others capabilities, the Yuan-ti is foiled several times by Ar’drus Gith-made armour and Ar’dru defeated in turn as his blade fails to pierce the creatures thick scales. Frustrated at the time being wasted Ar’dru lunges at the snake, blade again skittering across scale but again failing to penetrate, Ar’dru pays dearly for his rashness as the noble bites down hard into his shoulder. Conscious of the creatures formidable looking coils which begin to entangle him Ar’dru bites back and rolls away as the abomination rears up in pain, slashing at the creatures body as he turns back to face it.


The serpent withdraws slightly, tongue flickering as it watches Ar’dru intently. Ar’dru eyes it curiously and attempts to shuffle forward to put pressure on the giant but as he does so he discovers what his opponent has been waiting for, collapsing onto one knee as the poison steals the strength from his legs and clouds his vision. The serpent is on him in an instant, blade flashing down time and again, meeting only minimal resistance as Ar’dru attempts to angle his armour to protect himself as best he can from his current position.


Blinking his eyes repeatedly Ar’dru tries to clear his vision and his mind, resolving to face death as a warrior, not as a victim. As the serpent continues to rain down blows, somewhat more slowly now as the creature savours it’s dominance over the prone half-dragon, Ar’drus left hand shoots out, catching the Yuan-ti’s wrist mid-swing before trusting with a one handed grip at his opponents throat. The silver blade moves with such speed that the tip is needle fine at the point of impact until the blade once again flows forward, tearing open the wound and sealing the Yuan-ti’s fate. Ar’dru rolls back and hauls himself to his feet in time to watch the creature grasp feebly at its torn throat until, realising its own impending doom it calms, looks Ar’dru in the eye, and after giving him a respectful nod topples forward.


Ar’dru limps forward, looking down at the body. He sighs and with one powerful stroke removes the head, picking it up and fastening it to his belt. With that he turns to survey the rest of the field, taking flight if necessary to gain a better vantage point.
