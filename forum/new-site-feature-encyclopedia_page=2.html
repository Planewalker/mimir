I really like this encyclopedia project. I think it's shaping up well from what I've seen, and I might even write up a couple of entries if you guys are interested in unsolicited submissions.

One suggestion I had for the entries was to include the references to each subject in the official products. The Ravenloft Catalog on Kargatane.com was a great resource because it was amazingly documented with references to page numbers where the topic appeared in the literature. The one weakness of the Ravenloft Catalog was that it *only* contained page references and didn't actually describe the topic in question.

Words can't really do justice to the pedantic, cross-referency goodness of the Ravenloft Catalog. I encourage any of you guys to go to Kargatane.com and look up, say, 'Castle Ravenloft' to get an idea of just how thorough they were.

However, I realize that you guys are probably busy enough just getting the bugs worked out and writing the basic descriptions of the entries. So I'm volunteering to comb through the products to help dig up references as well as helping to write entries.
