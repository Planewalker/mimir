It feels as though I'm

It feels as though I'm prolonging the inevitable with this game. It feels as though we as a group are struggling to connect with a common motive and connection to the plot. This sort of conflict is nice in weekly tabletop games, where the pace suits it and you can fill a "what the hell do we do?"  session with something IC. We do not have the post rate to sustain that sort of thing, and while we may have at the very begining of the game, this is no longer the case. I need to seriously rethink how this game is planned and run if we're going to get anything done, because (prior to my untimely departure) we weren't. This is partly because I'm struggling to chalenge the different characters without splitting up the party, which is unsustainable. This is, I admit a rather depressing situation, but I'm going to try and rexamine things, rather than scrap everything just yet. This means that I really need your opinions.
