"Master", a new, creaky voice appears. "We should go inside. I am convinced those planewalkers are no match for us... or for you, that is. But who knows what else travels these areas. Inside, we have all the advantages on our side. Here, we do have vulnerabilities."

A few seconds pass, then Xanarox speaks again. "I thought I heard something. Damned fog..."

Another few seconds later, Xanarox replies to the other speaker. "Vulnerabilities. You powermongers are all alike. I wouldn't mind having an enemy who is a match for me, once only. All those missions bore me so much. Go inside."

For the first time, everyone in the group can clearly understand Xanarox. With the fog playing tricks with your senses, it takes a moment before you understand the reason of this change: Xanarox has come closer. He is no more than five yards away now.

As all this takes place, Saath feels a movement behind his back. He turns around, but all he sees is the fog moving in small whirls. Or... was that a monk's robe just disappearing?

While observing what is happening, Encharma suddenly feels a strange tickling in his head... no, in his mind. Something is looking into him. Scanning him. As from an infinite distance, he hears a whispering voice...
