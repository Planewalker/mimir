Re: Cross Purposes OOC

Oh, yeah.

Please roll skill checks in the IC thread. (You can keep the result of your current roll, Cadence.)

If you use a spell/power/whatever, mention that in the IC thread.

I like to enclose OOC text such as that in the IC thread using brackets like this.

[Knowledge:Arcana check below]

or something like that.

Or

[Read Thoughts]

You don't need to go and add that to your recent posts; just remember in future.

Syntax such as

OOC: Read Thoughts

works too.

The idea is to keep OOC talk in the IC thread to a minimum, but it's OK for skill rolls and similar things pertaining to the environment or game mechanics (such as asking questions about the manacles).
