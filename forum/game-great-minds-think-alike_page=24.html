Lady Sheeram gives Ophelia a wide smile. She walks over to her, and whispers for only her to hear: "Good to see you in a better mood, my friend. You are always a welcome guest at my house."

She then takes a place in the middle of the group. "I feel an urge in all of you to continue your mission. So, I will give you now what you have come here for."

Sheeram closes her eyes, then speaks in a monotone voice, "Three scribbling chests, containing documents from my last travel to Baator with Charrdh the Whisperer, pages three to seven, eighteen to twenty-two, and hundred to hundred-and-three."

Similar to how the table and chairs came up from the ground when the group entered, three small chests rise from the ground. They are wooden, with many protective steal plates and a silvery lock each. The chests appear exactly the same, a thumb's length high, a foot long and half a foot wide. The only differences are numbers on the chests' covers, displaying the pages that Sheeram spoke about. Those numbers are carved into the wood.

"I believe that these documents contain all the knowledge that will be of importance to you on your trip - unless none of you has Baator experience, but then I wouldn't recommend going down there, anyway."

She takes a look around the group, then addresses the Masked Gnoll. "I want you to take the first chest."
Turning to Blackthornes: "The second one is for you."
Finally, she gives a nod to Ophelia. "Please take the third chest."

After that, Sheeram silently waits for reactions.
