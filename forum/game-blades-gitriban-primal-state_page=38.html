Packing House - The

Packing House - The Offices

The trio of githyank warriors intercept the two members of the Meatpackers' Union, with deadly results. First, Gy-Nath emerges and gives the Kaasta a wound to the thigh. This does not kill the muscular reptile, but it gets his attention long enough for Du'minh to crush the planar lizardman's skull from behind with a powerful overhand swing. The halforc rounds the corner next, but before he can react to the gruesome scene, he is beheaded by Ii. Gy smiles in approval and turns to continue up the next flight of stairs when the gith hear a VERY loud noise from out on the killing floor, and are forced to duck and cover as broken glass fills the hallway.

Packing House - The Killing Floor

Ignoring the onrushing workers for now, Ar'dru quickly removes his pack and light a thunderblast grenade. He lobs it in a high arc towards the upper catwalk. It goes off just after the high point of its trajectory.

The first thunderblast back at the Hive Brothel was loud, loud enough to send echoes accross Sigil. The one in here has to compete with the bellows of still-frightened cattle, and the general din of a work. There is really no contest, however. There is a brilliant white flash and a bang that almost ruptures Ar'dru's eardrums. Most of the windows in the office 'shed' shatter. The herd of cattle panics anew, this time stampeding out of the Packing House to the best of their ability, crushing at least two startled workers in the process. The concussion knocks Foreman and his bodyguard flat, and one of them topples over the rail and falls to his death. Where the thunderblast went off, the catwalk's metal railing is broken and twisted. There is a rupture in the catwalk itself as well, not large, but considering the sturdy iron it was made of, showing the power of the alchemical grenade.

The Killing Floor Ears

The Killing Floor

Ears still ringing, Ar'dru retrieves his greatsword from the floor just as the first workers reach him. In these close quarters he grabs it by the handle with one hand and by the blade with the other. The workers soon regret their actions, as he shatters the cheekbone of the the first with the hilt, and cuts deeply into the torso of a second. Then they are on him, trying to weight him down with numbers. [roll vs grapple attempts. Evil DM in full effect says Ar'dru must beat the largest #, i.e. 25, which still won't be that hard, probably.]

Ya'shenn slides the exploding crystal across the floor, but though it is slick it also has several metal grates for the blood and offal to sluice through. The crystal takes a bad bounce as it hits one of these and tings loudly. Though it is likely none of the workers hear this, as their ears still ring from the thunderblast moments before, the crystal comes to rest at the feet of a large gnoll, who sees it and pauses. He even picks it up and looks around in the direction it came from, quickly spotting Ya'shenn. The gnoll draws out a large butcher knife still slick with cow blood and gives it a lick as he moves toward Ya'shenn (though he does not drop the crystal).

The Offices

Recovering swiftly and brushing broken glass off themselves, Gy, Du, and Ii climb the next set of stairs up to the second level of catwalks. They arrive just as Foreman is climbing painfully to its feet. Its bodyguard are either knocked out or killed by the concussion of the thunderblast genade. It sees the trio of githyanki in the doorway of the office 'building', and it eyes widen in fear. Not a race known for bravery, the illithid does the predictable: it tries to scurry away, first on all fours then grabbing a railing to get to its feet. The heavy wood and metal collar locked around the illithid's neck hampers it, however. Du'minh rushes forward and uses his newly-acquired magical mace to attack a support. Already weakened, it gives way, and a section of the catwalk drops a few feet before the other supports take up the slack. The point where the thundeblast ruptured the railing and deck gives way even more, pitching the now-sloping section sideways. Gy-Nath acts quickly once he sees what Du'minh is about to do. He yells to Ii to "Get down." and dives on top of her. Still close to the doorway, the pair are able to hold on during the ride.

[Du'minh has to pass a DC20 reflex save to do the same, or he'll fall. If he fails, he'll get a second chance, same DC, to land on the lower catwalk. Landing on the lower catwalk = d6 damage. Not landing on the lower catwalk = 3d6 damage]

Foreman pitches off the catwalk and falls all the way to the killing floor, landing about halfway between Ar'dru and Ya'shenn with a thud.
