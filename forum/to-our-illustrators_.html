To our illustrators.

If any illustrator/person who can actually draw (unlike me) would be interested in doing so, I would love to see illustrations of Tamer Creed mind flayers. For those who don't have copies of the Illithiad, they tend to be the front-line defenders of illithid communities, and (unlike most other mind flayers) specialize in physical combat, the better to deal with githyanki/zerai incursions and enemies that are resistant to mind blasts or brain extraction (i.e., undead, oozes, the psionically aware, those of unusually strong will). However, I offer no compensation for such illustrations other than "Wow! That's neat!"

Any kind of armor would work for such an illustration, with the following guidelines: Illithid armor is usually weird, and illithid armor generally includes some protection for the tentacles along with the rest of the body (though such armor would never prevent the tentacles from being used in combat). Some examples of illithid armor from various sourcebooks include: unusually lightweight plate armor (due to a combination of their generally superior 'technology' and ability of some of their crafters to psionically imbue items), light armor that literally bonds physically to the wearer's flesh and muscle, or even full exoskeletons. (But you can decide to draw totally different armor, too.) Almost any weapon/s would work also.

Enough of whining, time for yet another Artist comment:
When I was drawing this Ilithid firstly I wanted something something original (that is one standard I want to satisfy on every picture), so I was guided by 2 main ideas: what would uniforms of Ilithid guards on spelljamers look like? Since the Ilithid spelljamers are in shape of nautilus that was main idea for helmet shape.
Secondly, idea for the armour: Ilithids are clever oportunistic beings, so they dont hesitate from stealing other tehnologies from other species. For this armour idea was that unusual grell alchemy used for growing crystals. So for this crystaline armour ilithids probably used similar tehniques only on psionic crystals.

Thirdly: that weird worm thing Ilithid have instead of arm is probably some sort of graft created from grick and ilithid tadpole.

Ilithid is carrying psyonic spear, more as accessory and decoration rather than weapon (since the main wepon of ilithid is their tentacles), but sometimes you need to keep your enemies at bay and spear is perfect for that.

Finaly grimlock in the lover left part of the picture is there to fill space (and to assist its master in combat).
I have also put grimlock in picture as one other idea I recently had: you know that new story that Ilihids came fleeing from distant future where most suns are allready snuffed, so maybe grimlocks also came with Ilithids from that dark future to this time: blind slave race descended from humanoids (humans prehaps) adapted to live in universe without light. Think Morlocks from H.G.Wells Time Machine, only compleatly blind.
