This is a very general idea of Kion's (my character) history. The world this is set in is generic and eventually becomes a battlefield for Dragons:

Kion was the unwanted son of a Celestial (whom he never knew) and his mother, who, like most people, feared what she didn't understand which was what caused her to leave him. He was raised in a remote village as a normal boy, a farmer's son. And though his silver eyes were out of the ordinary, he managed to fit in. He was never told of his heritage. Though he figured it out for himself later in life.

He was raised in a neutral environment, where any kind of small good and bad deeds were done by the villagers, though it was always balanced slightly towards the good. Kion always felt a natural urge to break this balance as much as possible towards good.
This was a very clear trait of his all of his life and he continued religiously believing in good, until he moved to one of the great cities in the world, where he began acting as a squire to a Paladin.

He was very close to becoming a Paladin himself when it happened; Dragons raged the earth and fought- good against evil. Many Humans perished in this battle, though they prayed and fought, but Kion managed to survive. He was awed by the Dragons' power and immediately attempted to join the efforts of the silver and gold Dragons, which where considered to be of the Good alignment. Kion gave up his belief in the gods, who failed his people when they needed them the most and instead moved his efforts to the Dragons, creating a pact with them and therefore becoming a Gold Dragon Shaman.

Um, I was thinking that he will be commanded by the Dragon Spirits to go to Sigil later on, but this is where I am at so far.

Concerning personality and goals in life:

First off, Kion sees that world as black and white, with perhaps a shade of gray in between, but basically- to him it's either good or evil.

Kion is extreme in his alignment, and while he wont always do what it takes to fulfill his ideal world (though he's well on his way to be capable on anything), which is why he became a Paladin from the beginning.

As a child he was left by his Celestial father and Human mother, something that caused a conflict in his youth- He did not trust or truly want to align himself with higher powers such as gods or celestials, who seem (to Kion, anyway) to consider humans as nothing as a result and believed that his mother was simply partially evil. This belief only increased his desire to fix society into his ideal.

Upon becoming a Dragon Shaman, he did not change at all, these powers were simply a way for him to fulfill his goals.
