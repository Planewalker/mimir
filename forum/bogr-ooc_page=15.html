OK, in case the others haven't thought of this hypothetical scenario:

Let's say our tentacled friend is actually up there. In these conditions:

* can't pepper it with missile fire
* can't surround and overwhelm it in melee
* perfect setup for mind blasting all four of us at once
* it mostly doesn't have to fight more than one of us at a time
* it also still has an avenue of retreat in case things go wrong

Basically, it would be playing to all of its strengths and none of ours. It would be inconsistent with the claws, though. (It could even be our flayer 'friend' plus a thrall creature up there. If it's just the thrall creature/beast/whatever, we'll mostly have an easier time of things. Probably.)
