Behind the bar, the door to Charrdh's rooms opens. The barkeeper hurries to step to the right edge of the bar, as far away from the door as possible.

A dark figure walks up from the stairway: Dead skin on a thin, bony body, a grinning face with large fangs and eye sockets filled with glowing yellow lights (and no eyes). The creature wears a bejeweled robe, tattered by probably a couple of centuries, and a large king's crown on his bald head. The humanoid is about seven feet tall, and appears strong and powerful despite the almost skeletal appearance.

He is followed closely by another creature, resembling him almost like a twin. The main difference is the fine, pitch black robe that the second humanoid wears, and the gnarled staff he holds which crackles with blue magical energy.

The two beings position themselves at each side of the door. The one on the right pushes his staff on the ground with a loud Slam!, then announces in a creaky voice:

"Free a path for Xanarox, apprentice, ambassador and personal assassin of the great Juiblex, traveller of time and traitor extraordinaire. Those who stand in his way will suffer a terrible death."

As the creature finishes his speech, a young man - no more than sixteen years old - comes up the stairway. His light brown clothes are fine, but not outstanding, as is his entire appearance: He could fit in almost everywhere, from Hive to Lady's Ward, from the Abyss to Mt. Celestia. The most outstanding thing about him is that there appears to be absolutely nothing outstanding about him.
