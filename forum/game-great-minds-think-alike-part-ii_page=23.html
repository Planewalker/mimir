Ian pulls the ring, and the heavy stone door slides upwards, disappearing in a shaft in the ceiling.

Behind that, a circular room opens up, about ten yards in diameter. Three humanoid corpses lie on the floor - shredded to pieces so that it's impossible to say what races they were.

A yard behind, Blackthornes' puppy lies on the floor - obviously dead, with a gaping hole at its belly.

The walls are covered with plants and trees that have been planted into the ground - which consists of black soil. There are many spots here where someone could hide behind one of the plants.

The room's ceiling is about six yards high, made of large plates of glass that are covered in green and red twines, many of them displaying large and colourful blossoms.

I was all you wanted me to be... something to protect - , suddenly, a golden flash appears in the Masked Gnoll's bag, where his animal companion rests, a friend that you don't need to care about - , another flash appears illuminating Blackthornes for a split second, a mystery, a danger to the group - , two more flashes for Naruk and Azure, and now I am even dead. The last flash engulfs Ian in golden light.

I could have been everything, a companion, a true friend, even a powerful ally... but the best you wished for was not knowing me at all.
