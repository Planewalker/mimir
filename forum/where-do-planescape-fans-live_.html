you're associating search trends in google for a specific term with fanbase, which is probably a pretty poor proxy. Note that the same google trends calculator when fed D&D gives Italy as the number one, Forgotten Realms nets Hungary as number one. Interesting Dungeons and Dragons, spelled out as oppossed to the acronym, gives the US and Canada number one, so does Eberron.

I have a feeling that this tracker uses exactitude and the spellings of the same terms in other languages register differently (Yao Ming returns China as number 7 for example) and so a term like planescape may skew towards international for such reasons.
