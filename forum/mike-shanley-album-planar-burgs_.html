A few years ago, a musician named Michael Shanley or Mike Shanley produced an album called Planar Burgs, consisting of tracks inspired by locations in Planescape, and he very kindly released it under a Creative Commons license and made it free to download. Unfortunately, Shanley has since dropped off the face of the internet and his site no longer exists. Did anyone here happen to download his album, and, if so, any chance of making it available for the rest of us?
