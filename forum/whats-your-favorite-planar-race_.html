What's your favorite planar race?

Submitted by Krypter on Thu, 2005-01-06 20:56
in
  • RPG Discussion

Planescape is known for several iconic races, one of which became so popular that it was crudely shoe-horned into the third edition Forgotten realms Campaign Setting. Can you guess what race that was?

Personally, I like the yugoloths and tieflings the most. The 'loths are evil and demonic-looking, but turn out to be surprisingly human in behaviour. They're selfish, greedy, mercenary and callous, just like some people can be.

Tieflings, on the other hand, look somewhat demonic but are really just mischievous rebels. They're people, but with strange bodily quirks and a lot of attitude. They've got sass and plenty of cunning from their diluted infernal blood.

What's your favorite race?

PS: I couldn't fit any more options into this poll, so if your favorite race is missing, my apologies. No Xills, Nathri, Ethergaunts, Ratatosk, Gehreleths, Thokola, Neogi or Tso.
