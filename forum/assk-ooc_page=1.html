Wasn't 100% clear on the room set-up.

The room is a fairly large dorm, with about a dozen beds and a common area on one side. There are no windows, the only light coming from three large lanterns hanging from chains in the ceiling. They are too high to reach without a ladder, and are probably magical so never need refueling.

There is a small bathroom separate from the main dorm, with an entrance near the common area. It contains a 'seat' (think outhouse hole, but likely much deeper) and a sink. There is a drain in the sink, and a waterpipe that constantly trickles (surprisingly clean) water, filling the bathroom with a soothing sound. There is no door between the batroom and the common dorm, but there is a curtain on a rod bolted firmly to the stone.

There are no cells or bars, the door to the dorm is thick wood, with two ports; one eye-level and one at the base of the door so the keepers can slide food trays in without opening the door. Each port has a sliding panel, the one at eye level also has a couple of bars to prevent a reach-through (though Igo is small enough that his arm might fit)

I don't know if you all got the 'potato' joke there. The potato pancakes and potato stew are a nod to the 'Potato King of Sigil' a barmy berk whose ill-worded wish won him an ever-full sack of spuds (the exact wording of what his wish was is an ongoing topic of discussion throughout Sigil, I'm sure). He mostly walks around with it upended, leaving a trail of potatoes down the street, and is known not to be stingy when it comes to giving out free spuds. Thus, potatoes are a large part of the Sigilan diet, and since they tend to be cheap or free, and because the Potato King himself lives in the Hive, the poorer one is the larger percentage of one's diet is potatoes.
