Lady of Pain Costume

Hello everyone!

I've just started here on the forum, but I've been an avid Planescape fan for years....and I've finally decided to make a costume that comes from the campaign. I'm starting to work on a puppet/costume of the Lady of Pain. 

 I made this costume http://xbc.xanga.com/9bca6266c453270616566/b47481774.jpg a while ago, and shortly after that I made this one of an undead warlock http://i281.photobucket.com/albums/kk228/Fyriel/n61011942_33609285_4397.jpg and this one of some crazy steampunk...thing http://i281.photobucket.com/albums/kk228/Fyriel/portpage6.jpg 

(you can see more at my portfolio, www.fyriel.com)

and now, I'm working on an 8 foot tall (or more) version of the Lady of
Pain. So, since you're all amazing and love planescape as much as I do
(at least most of you) I thought you'd enjoy watching me agonize over
making this insane costume...and if you have any
support/comments/criticism, yell at me!

so go to my blog...http://sorceryandglue.blogspot.com

and watch me make it!  I'm hoping or it to be done by the
end of July for comic con, so there will be lots of updates...and of
course, if you know anything about planescape and anything I can add to
the costume, do tell. If anything, I'm hoping the costume will do this amazing campaign justice.



Thanks everyone!
