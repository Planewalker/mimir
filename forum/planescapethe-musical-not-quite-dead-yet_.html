Planescape:the musical, Not quite dead yet

The basic outline is fairly simple, everyone has bard levels and randomly breaking out into song and dance is encouraged. The goal here is to simply have fun and give the bard class some much needed attention. I have also come up with 2 feats that would be fitting for this type of game. If people don't want another freeform game I'd be willing to DM.

THEME SONG[GENERAL]
Prerequisites:Cha 13.
Benefit:Whenever a sufficiently dramatic situation occurs(determine by a DM) a song chosen at the time you take this feat will play from either thin air or be played by something nearby(making it look like a coincidence), this song grants the effects of a 1st level bard's inspire courage song.

MUSICAL[GENERAL]
Prerequisites:CHA 15, theme song,bard level 4.
Benefit:As a full-round action you can make everyone within 50 feet make a will save(DC=your perform check) or begin to dance and sing along with you as if in a musical. This feat acts as the bard's fascinate song except as noted above.

The second one is a bit messy, suggestions on how to improve the feats are welcome. If this is to become a full fledged game it would start at level 5 and all non-core material would need to be checked, planewalker material is allowed.

PS: The Musical! LOL! Okay, here's my 2 greens...

SOLILOQUY [GENERAL]
Prerequisites: CHA 13, Theme Song
Benefit: You can suspend time and freeze the actions of all beings within a 50 ft. radius. Time remains suspended for the duration of the oration or song, but resumes as if no interruption had taken place once the Soliloquy ends. During the Soliloquy, you may attempt to influence the thoughts and feelings of a single being within range. That individual must make a Will save vs. your Perform check or be affected as if by the Suggestion spell.

For the duration of the Soliloquy, your personal Theme Song will be heard as if from a distance and all lights within range will dim to half their normal brightness.

DOUBLE ENTENDRE [GENERAL]
Prerequisites: INT 13, CHA 13
Benefit: With your razor-sharp wit and extensive vocabulary, you can garner popular favour at the expense of a target of your choosing. Your target must make a Will save vs. your Perform check. If you succeed, you will gain a +1 Circumstance bonus on all interractions with witnesses to the exchange for 24 hours, while your target suffers a -1 Circumstance penalty.
