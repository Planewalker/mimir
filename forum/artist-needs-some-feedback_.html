Size wise - I'd suggest that it be sized enough to be seen on a 1280x 1028 screen at least (maybe a little smaller to allow for space for the webpage to show). That'll cut down the memory size considerably as well - but for further trimming consider that on the screen you don't need the 300 - 400 dpi that you would need for a book, so you can scan something in at a resolution that's easier on the memory as well. And of course jpg and gif is smaller than bmp.
