OK folks, I beat Evil DM at his own game, he's hog-tied in the closet now. The reason for this is, that I want to give you a few clues, and expose just how Evil he is.

So here is the 'take 20' on Ya'shenn's 'Knwoledge: Psi' check.

Thralldom -

There are three levels of true thralldom, not just being a slave but mentally dominated by the illithids to a degree where one remains entralled even when not in their masters' presence.

The Empty - Psionicly lobotomized. Most often seen in thrall soldiers.

The Subtle - These are the subconciously dominated. The illithids use a focus, either behavioral or physical. It might be an affinity for an object, a color, a phrase, or a state of mind. Some things can be used, while others can't. A ghaik can't dominate a githyanki, and have the focus be a blade, or a line of Gith's scripture repeated over and over. Githzerai wear grey, because grey and silver can't be used as sunconcious foci, too neutral. Basicly, the 'arms race' between the ghaik and githyanki psions is played out in devising and detecting new and more subtle foci.

The Marked - A ghaik actually dissolved it's way into the victim's body and physicaly modified their nerves. There is one sure way of detecting the mark: find the scar. Beyond that, all bets are off, because this is a practice that is unique to the illithids, and a powerful one at that. The most common marks do common things, of course, but they can be quite powerful. For instance, Jhank'kor's mark (BTW Gy-Nath is a psion too, if he didn't want to show Jhank'kor's mark to Ya'shenn, he wouldn't have) is "Pain", but it is not only used to keep thralls in line, but to make them into thrall overseers by being able to project their pain.
