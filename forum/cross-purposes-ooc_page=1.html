I think it works as follows:

You'd have to drop Read Thoughts in order to initiate Mindlink, just because Read Thoughts requires constant concentration. However, one could do it in the reverse order: Set up a Mindlink, then initiate Read Thoughts, because a mindlink does not require concentration after it has been established.

Mindlink also requires that the people being mindlinked are willing.

A note about the Psionic Domination my character is attempting:

The Half Orc is being dominated for a day/level (4 additional power points) and the additional target is the nondescript (2 additional power points for an additional target). Thus, according to the description, the powers DC goes up by 3 due to expenditure of 6 additional power points.

So the total DC for the save of the attempted Domination:

10+4(Intelligence modifier) +2 (feats) +4 (Power level) +3 (Additional) = 23
