Character Creation Rules and House Rules
* Organic Ability Score Generation
* Material Available: Core Rulebooks, Pathfinder Alpha Release 2, Expanded Psionics Handbook, & the Planescape Setting. I could possibly be convinced to include something if I have the book, but I'd prefer to stick with these books.
* Characters will be level 1. We will be using Unearthed Arcana 's Bloodline rules for Planetouched. Some races have modified Level Adjustments these include: Bariaur: +0, Bladeling: +0, Githyanki: +0, Githzerai: +1. We will be using Dragon Magazine's Modron, so you can use the Exiled Modron race, however they will be only LA +0.
* We will be using Traits and Flaws from Unearthed Arcana.
* We will be using Weapon Group Feats from Unearthed Arcana instead of Weapon Profiencies, and for weapon feats.
* We will be using Magic Ratings from Unearthed Arcana. However there will be seperate Arcane, Divine, Martial, and Psionic Ratings. The Martial Rating applies to when you gain Uncanny Dodge and Improved Uncanny Dodge, Diamond Mind and class-based saving throws for extraordinary abilities. The Divine Rating applies to Turn Undead.
* Class Skills for each Class (Int Bonus excluded): Barbarian: 4 , Bard: 6, Cleric: 4, Druid: 4, Fighter: 4, Monk: 4, Paladin: 4, Ranger: 6, Rogue: 8, Sorcerer: 4,
Wizard: 4.
* Class Skills for the Fighter are: Acrobatics, Climb, Craft, Deception, Diplomacy, Handle Animal, Heal, Insight*, Intimidate, Knowledge (dungeoneering, engineering, geography, history, local, nobility), Perception, Perform, Profession, Ride, Survival, Swim. *Insight includes Sense Motive and Gather Information and is Wisdom based.
* We're using the Constitution variant for starting hit points.
* If you want to play a variant from Unearthed Arcana or to design a new variant to play ask me.
* A Fighter's Weapon Training ability adds +1 to attacks and +2 to damage per level of Weapon Training. So a 9th level Fighter who choose axes at 5th level and bows at 9th would have a +2 to atks with axes and +1 with bows and + 4 to axe damage and +2 to bow damage.
* Monks get d10 HD and Good BAB. They can use any weapon they are proficient in for Flurry of Blows.
* No alignment requirements for base classes, except for Paladins. Variant alignment Paladins are available from Unearthed Arcana.
* Trapfinding is a feat, requiring Disable Device or Craft (trapsmith) 1 rank.
* http://paizo.com/.../simpleElegantAndOPTIONALSkillSynergies This rule is being used.
* Levels in a favored class give 1 skill point per level as well as hit points.

A couple more House Rules:

* No multiclass restrictions
* Clerics get d6, Poor BAB, Poor Fort Saves, Proficiency in Weapon Group (basic weapons), one additional Weapon Group, and Light Armor. If you want to play a 3/4 HD Cleric we can figure out a variant.
* Clerics and Druids are spontaneous casters as Unearthed Arcana, and can learn any spell previously associated with the Domains of they're deity.

Jarmo dSivis of Korranberg

Although Jarmo now presents himself as some kind of a Prime world noble, he was born to a hiver gnome family. His fiendish appearance made him the scapegoat for his siblings. Every time one if his brothers broke something, or something was missing, Jarmo got the blame.

He slowly, but steadily grew to despise his family, and the abysmal living conditions of the Hive. He decided that life must hold something more for him, so he set out to find it. Ever since childhood, Jarmo has had the knack for spontaneously casting spells, even more so than most gnomes. Through theft, trickery and some research, he has re-imaged himself as some kind of Prime noble hailing from the relatively unknown world of Eberron. He claims to be a member of a Dragonmarked house Sivis, and has managed to get a hold of the houses signet ring. The ring and the nice clothes have depleted his resources momentarily, so currently Jarmo is on a lookout for some well-paying cons and jobs.

Besides the fancy colorful clothes Jarmo is wearing, his most notable feature is his small but sharp claws on his hands. More perceptive observers can spot Jarmos forked tongue as well.
