The fiend, grotesque as it seems, is obviously female, packed with muscles and has four large curled horns on its head.

“Now see my true form,” she speaks in a dark – yet strangely female – voice, “and experience agony beyond your imagination as I swallow your souls.”

Flames ignite all over the fiend’s body, and as Liria – if this creature still is Liria – stomps her hooves on the street, the ground ignites as well.

The Harmonium Officers stare at her in total shock – until their leader grabs one of his men, shouting, “Get us support over here! Run, RUN!”
The man doesn’t hesitate a single second, and he obviously runs as fast as he can.

The remaining succubi move to Liria’s left and right side – it seems like they plan to leave the battle to her now.

The fiend’s face looks like a grotesque version of Liria’s beauty, hard, bony and twisted, and the large yellow eyes stare at everyone with intense hatred.
