Most of the races here in Kazzex are humanoid, although few of them are easily recognisable as planar races you're familiar with. Tieflings, though, tend to have quite a myriad amount of appearances...

The guide leads you all through the city, occasionally pointing to one side or the other. It doesn't take long to learn that these gestures serve as a warning, and within a few seconds of her gesture that piece of the city moves away, or a swinging piece of metal arcs through the area, or what looks like an apparently sentient pack of large gears rolls through.

After almost twenty minutes of such almost-perilous travel, she arrives at a large building. The sign says, in a number of languages (one of them being Planar Trade), 'Guides, Touts, and Escorts: Easy and Cheap Survival in the Spinning City'. She inclines her head towards the door, standing next to it expectantly.
