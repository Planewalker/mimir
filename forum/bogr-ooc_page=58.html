Zimrazim wrote:

Question for Azure:  Is there only one Queen's Necromancer, or is there one for each Incursion world, or...?

Neither.  It's a title, nothing more (but nothing less, since it is a rather impressive title).  There would not have been one in each Incursion, but many would have had one.  Figure that it means he was a member of an inner circle with about 8 to 12 others of the same title.  Of the nine major Incursion worlds, probably six or so had a Queen's Necromancer, depending on the overall strategy.  Saev, for example, (where Ya'shenn served) did not.

It is an impressive title, though.  The chain of command for Toril's Incursion went; Overlord, Queen's Champion, Queen's Mage, Queen's Necromancer, Supreme Leader of Knights, Master Gish, etc. .... so #4 in command and still higher than the highest ranking Knight.
