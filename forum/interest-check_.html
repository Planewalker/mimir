Interest check?

This is basically just me asking if anyone would like to join a 3.5 game I am planning. I like to put spins on things, primaraly homebrew content, which always in my opinion makes things more fun.

Mainly I want to see if anyone is interested in a game where we play as outsiders instead of the normal races. (archons, modrons, slaad, eladrin, angels, devils elementals, ect) The main problem with playing these races is level adjustment is way to high. Thus I propose this game has a new way of playing level adjustment where your ECL is simply the creatures CR+1.

Also I would allow homebrew races and feats and such, provided they aren't too overpowered.

Some good homebrew feats here: http://www.dnd-wiki.org/wiki/Book_of_Elements_%283.5e_Sourcebook%29/Elem...

Hope to see some interest here.

Do you have any more info on your game to share? Not looking for spoilers or anything of the like, but something so that any prospective players have some idea what they're getting into. Permissible races, an alternate process for dealing with annoying level adjustments, and maximum allowable PCs per player are there, and that's a good start.
What sort of game do you intend to run? Are you looking for a serious game? A "just for fun, go crazy, do whatever you want" kind of game? Or will it depend on the characters?
Since you're proposing outsider PCs, I assume it's a Planscape setting? If so, would it be pre- or post-faction war?
What character alignment would best suit your game? Or are any and all permissible?
What about starting location? So your players will be able to write into their backstory their characters' motivations for coming to be there.

A lot of questions, I know. But you catch more fish with a baited hook. And it should help other prospective players determine more quickly if they're interested.
