In the long distant but not forgotten past, there were two netzines produced by the Planescape community. We in fact have archives of both of them on the site here:

S.I.G.I.S.
The Lady's Sharper Eye

Now that we've gotten ourselves back together, I asked recently on the mailing lists and livejournal groups that the community sprawls across, if we would like to restart one of these mags. The response was an enthusiatic "YES!" - so here we are.

I've volunteered to play 'cat-herder', so I'll be the primary person that submissions and editing will go through. I'm hoping that the writers from previous version of SIGIS and The Lady's Sharper Eye will bear with me as I get the hang of working with you and getting this mag out the door. In the meantime, I'm here to field most any question you have - be you new blood or an old hand to this.

Submissions to the Lady's Sharper Eye can be sent to me via PM, or to : [REDACTED]

This forum here on Planewalker is a 'touch point' for the continuation of this newsrag. Here is where I'll be the easiest to contact, and I hope all the diverse members of all the various sprawling groups will be able to come here as a central location to ask questions and make submissions.

I'd like to get our writers in the habit of putting in articles three weeks in advance of when the issue they're submitting for is to be released.

That'll give our artists and myself plenty of time to apply art and editting to the articles.

This means for example:
For an issue out on July 1st.
Writing needs to be submitted by June 10th give or take.
