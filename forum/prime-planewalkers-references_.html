Re: Prime planewalkers references

These guys were already very famous in their own settings, or at least believe themselves to be. Then they planewalk their self-important selves into Sigil and suddenly they're nobodies again. No squealing fans, no autographs and everybody looks at you all weird like you've ended up in the wrong part of town. That's not very fun.

Another reason may be that a whole bunch of them planewalked (read: got kidnapped) themselves into a lot of trouble and ended up in a lower plane before their time. Eventually most of them escaped, but I doubt they made a lot of friends there. Not even pen pals.

Those that didn't leave did actually make quite a name for themselves.
