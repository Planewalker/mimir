Schools are gone at least in the format they were in 3e, as they tried too hard to keep those schools balanced and found that they couldn't. So specialist wizards aren't going to be anything like 3e specialists. They're going to be completely separate classes like the warmage, dread necromancer and beguiler introduced later in 3.5e.

But for one thing I didn't like there being all these enchantment spells when I had a psion around, simply because they kept on ignoring the psion and then giving other classes abilities better than areas the psion was supposed to be at.

Wizards still get spells like charm person and various necromancy spells, but the bulk of those spells are going to the psion or the bard, or the new "shadow power source" classes that are like necromancers and illusionists.
