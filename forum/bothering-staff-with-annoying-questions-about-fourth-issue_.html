Ok - a brief summation of the job such as it is.

You get your submissions and hound people to get more to you. Then you edit them into something resembling comprehensible dialect influenced english. Then you find images to go with them, and ads to go with them with nifty text. Then you crack open word (or whatever your layout program is wont to be) and assemble them. In word I've found text boxes linked together to be helpful to allow for text to flow from one page to the next. Use ads to make up for 'holes' in your layout. And eventually - if your copy of word hasn't ate it's face off - you use the 'Print as PDF' feature of your copy of Adobe to make a PDF of it.

That's very boiled down mind you, and subject to change if you have better skills at such things than I do. Really - the hard part is all in word (or whatever layout program you prefer to use) making a nice layout of the articles. Making it a PDF is fairly simple if you have a working full copy of adobe acrobat. You just hit the button to make it do it's magic.
