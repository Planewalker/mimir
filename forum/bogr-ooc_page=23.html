OK, it might be an odd character concept, but bear with me.

The Githyanki Bard: Bardic tradititons of the githyanki are different from that of other races. The githyanki bard is expected to be a scout and a lone operative behind enemy lines. They are loremasters, but also jacks-of-all-trades, knowing some sorcery, some swordplay, some stealthiness, and many other things. They might be likened somewhat to Athasian 'bards' who are expected to be as good of assassins as they are musicians. Unlike many other bards, githyanki do not spread knowledge, rather they keep it until they report to their commander, at which point their highly-trained memory and observation skills come into play. One way in which they are similar to other races' bards is in their use of music. Many githyanki instruments are unique, and some cannot even be played properly without an innate telekinetic ability.

Yes I really do sit around coming up with character concepts. The githyanki bard isn't going to be anywhere near as interesting as the (insane) illithid with bard levels that will be coming along, I think.
