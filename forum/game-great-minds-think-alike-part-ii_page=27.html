As Blackthornes opens the door, what he sees is not what he remembers from his vision. In a cubic room with merely a single door on the opposite side, a strange creature floats in mid-air: The upper half is an ice-blue humanoid being, but where the legs should be, there is merely a whirling miniature tornado.

The muscular creature raises its sharp claws, and stares at you with a hateful grin. But before it can attack, the room suddenly shakes - and quickly moves downwards. You barely see the creature's surprised face before it is replaced by a massive wall, which then, as it moves further downward, gives place for another corridor.

The corridor is dark, and after a few yards, end in a staircase that leads steeply downward.

The footsteps are closing in, and will probably reach the corridor that the group is in now in a few seconds.
