Psionic Drugs

I myself am a huige fan of psionics. I have used them in my games ever since 1st edition. BEfore you post that stuff let me check on whether the drug rules in BoVD are open content or not. If not we cannot use them legally. Now you might be able to put something together using the intoxication rules from the Arms and Equipment Guide, but I have yet to compare the two systems and am not sure how close they are.

Whatever the decision, please be careful how you present the subject. I myself am nearly impossible to offend, but there are many who get in an uproar whenever drugs, sexuality, or RL religions are brought into gaming. Just look at all the vitriol on the net about Valar Press and their erotic d20 efforts.

We are a site that attracts people of all ages, and in order to retain our status must be careful. I am not trying to discourage you (not at all!), but rather pointing out some of the pitfalls of this type of entry.

Always looking forward to your work, Nemui!

Rules 'N Fluff

I tend to like both a lot. That is one reason that I enjoy 3.5 DND. The rules and crunchy aspects are streamlined and allow for infinite customization giving my PCs and I guidleines on how to rapidly resolve any die rolling situation. HAving things so logical and stackable allows me more time for the fun part- storytelling!

Of course in my games every player knows that just because it is a rule does not mean I won't make a DM Ruling tht throws it out in a particular instance. Story is the objective, rules are simply a roadmap to getting there and fluff is the stuff you remember doing on the trip. All are aimportant.

I would like to encourage fluff as the majority of WOTC and other publishers seem to be focusing on crunch only.
