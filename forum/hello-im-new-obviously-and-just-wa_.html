Thank you I really appreciate the comment.
Most of them were done with pencil then finished in either Painter Pro or Open Canvas, using the airbrush and blender tools. The guy in the post above was done with my trial version of Photoshop (omg that is a hard one to work with) but he was done with traditional soft airbrush, blending tools, and then I used the Posterize filter to give him a kinda comic book look.
It's a process but I rather like blending and coloring, it makes things like muscle lines subtle.
