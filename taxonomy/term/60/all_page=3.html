Githwarrior Champion

Submitted by Mephit James on Wed, 2004-12-15 02:00
in
  • Prestige Class
  • Astral
  • Rrakkma: Legacy of Zerthimon

While many are familiar with the powerful githyanki Silver Knights, the majority of githyanki soldiers follow the somewhat less prestigious career path of githwarrior. This catch-all term for non-knightly githyanki combatants is used to describe the rank and file of githyanki armies, the front line that the Silver Knights direct in combat against the enemies of the lich queen.
