Personality: On the almost unheard-of plane known as Kal-Thyra, a shadowy figure slips silently through the trees. He is at one with the forest around him, and is seen and heard only if he wishes to be. He is a Thyrian, a cat-like race known for its stealth and agility.Thyrians are strongly attached to nature. They understand the world around them, and can make peace with it easily. They are skilled hunters, and can get all they need from the land. They avoid busy areas, like town markets and such, because they are very uneasy in crowds.
