Tongue Charm

Submitted by Anonymous on Sat, 2004-11-20 02:00
in
  • Magical Item
  • Equipment

This charm is a small, shrivelled tongue, obviously obtained from some humanoid creature. Its roughly textured surface varies in colour dramatically, from a rusty flesh colour through to a bluish white. Green and other exotically toned tongue charms are often used, clearly signalling the wide variety of creatures from which tongues are obtained to be prepared for the magical process. Across the dried surface of the tongue, barely visible runes can be distinguished, having either been painted onto it or stitched into the charm.

Indep Earring

Submitted by Carnifex on Sat, 2004-11-20 02:00
in
  • Magical Item
  • Equipment

This barbed earring marks the wearer as a member of the Indep faction. This earring is the home of an independent spirit which accompanies the wearer and does its best to protect him from harm. Whenever possible, the spirit gives its wearer a mental nudge to get them out of harm's way... the tiny counselling spirit provides the user with a +1 resistance bonus to all saving throws and a +1 dodge bonus to AC, and its mere presence gives the wearer an additional bonus to avoid spells (additional +1 bonus). The wearer also becomes immune to Confusion.

Harmonium Whistle

Submitted by Anonymous on Sat, 2004-11-20 02:00
in
  • Magical Item
  • Equipment

This small bronze whistle, nicknamed "the long arm of the law", is a common implement amongst Harmonium officers. When the whistle is blown, the piercing blast invokes a Hold Monster spell on the target miscreant while also alerting nearby Harmonium guards. Whistles of this type are most often used in capturing defiant criminals who consistently try to flee from arrest and evade their pursuers – while held, they can be easily subdued and handcuffed.
