The Locals Teardrop VineCopyright © 2000 by Leonardo Wilhelm The Teardrop Vine, named after the realm, has strange little red fruits similar to tears that have strange properties. Anyone who eats said fruits, that seems to grow even from the lava, has terrible hallucinations of a grim fate where the victim victimizes some loved one for his actions and brings about pain. Many fiends like to drop these fruits in celestial drinks.
