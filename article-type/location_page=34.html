El Ziafar

Submitted by ripvanwormer on Thu, 2005-04-28 01:00
in
  • Location
  • Air

Long before there was a Citadel of Ice and Steel, there was El Ziafar. Once upon a time the djinn had no single leader, but were broken into many tribes even as the jann are today. It came to pass that eight tribes settled in a great axial point in the Plane of Air where eight winds diverged in eight directions. Each of the eight claimed one of the winds and built a gate to harness it and a palace around the gate. Eventually the palace-gates were connected and became a city.
