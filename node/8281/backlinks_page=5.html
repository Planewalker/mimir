As he enters, he hears a distant buzzing sound, as if rain is falling. The store itself seems small for the large size of the building it's in. It's a dark violet themed store. Magical candles provide subtle, though eerie. illumination. On the right wall are wands, rings, pendants, and other enchanted jewelry. On the left wall are assorted body parts, well preserved (and lacking the smell of the stash outside). Once again Edahs sees Edahs...or Sselp Leh- that SAME man working on stacking potions and scrolls on the middle shelf. He gives Edahs...himself, a short glance, then continues working.

To the right side of the store is something that seems out of place - a Suit of antique armor with an Axe attached to the right gauntlet. The Suit stands upright with no supports, as if the wearer is invisible. But ultimately, the half dragon decides that there is no wearer - because the suit doesn't budge.

In front of the small shop is a counter with skulls and more scrolls, along with a stacked bookshelf on the far corner. Standing behind the counter is the most unusual of .... people.
