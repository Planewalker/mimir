New Game: "Final Judgement"

Ok, I've got two news.

The good one is, I'm starting a new game.
The bad one is, it's already full.

Five players will show up to learn that being dead in a bad afterlife is no bed of roses. The game will start in few weeks, now is the time for players send me their characters.

Rules for character creation:
- only basic rules from PHB I and II allowed, feats and spells from other supplements (Complete series) are subject to approval of the DM.
- prestige classes are forbidden, except direct approval of the DM. Non-core basic classes as hexblade, knight, favoured soul and spirit shaman allowed, except the warlock.
- the aligment of the party should incline to good or neutral, no bad boys.
- the characters remember their past, but their mind is clouded with strange fog... As a background, you all may choose three most notable memories you possess - one from childhood, one from the time you became an adventurer, one from times you were an adventurer.
(NOTE: The memories chosen to be remembered should reflect your aligment and the way your character acts / thinks, maybe what moments of his life made him the person he is now)
- the party members know the other characters (more or less - they cannot really remember it correctly...), the background of the group and how they got together will be discussed with the players individually.
- characters start at lvl 3 (low level campaign) or equivalent, if races with ECL are chosen.
- players will contact me for further info (most of them via icq, probably).
