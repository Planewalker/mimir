A whirling wind of darkness is all that Blackthornes sees for a moment. Sees, feels, even smells. Everything is gone.

Even he...

This must be the end. His heart feels cold, his soul slowly fading into non-existence, all his emotions unimportant, even his pain. His pain... it fills his mind, every little pain he ever had to feel. He observes it as from a far point, as if he was outside of his own body.

Every little pain...

The darkness has turned into a maelstrom, and it feels like it is crushing Blackthornes, like grinding stones turning his very existence into dust. It feels impossible to fight, the weight of pain and the strength of the maelstrom too much to bear.

Still... there is this voice. This voice from deep inside of his heart...

Yes, your heart, Thornes says. His cat-like voice brutally pulls Blackthornes out of his trance, the reality and danger of the situation turning back into the tiefling's consciousness. Focus on your heart. Let it lead you.

He hears the pounding of his heart, and he realizes all his feelings are still there; they were not gone, they were just too numb to feel.

Suddenly, he falls, backwards, out of the darkness, away from the grinding maelstrom, faster and faster...
