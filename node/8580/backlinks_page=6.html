Her head looks like that of a wolf or jackal's but with the edges smoothed out and the features less clearly defined. The large and glowing eyes are sunken slightly into her head and the sockets are surrounded by a blue half circle painted onto her fur. She has an extremely large mane of hair running down to her shoulders with a longer ponytail held that way by a steel cylinder.
