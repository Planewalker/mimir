The GM's decision goes on this one, Jem, but my understanding of monster classes and LA is that monster HD count as full class levels (often crappy class levels, mind you, but still class levels). Your character would accordingly be a centaur Ftr4 - 4 'levels' of monstrous humanoid, +2 LA, 4 class levels for a total ECL of 10.

If this were not the case, a player could come to an ECL 10 game with, say, a Very Young Copper Dragon PC (8 dragon HD, LA +3) and seven levels of Barbarian on top, and have 15d12 hit dice, a +15 BAB, flight, exceptional senses and a couple of breath weapons. Shouldn't be hard to see why the rules as written consider such a character to be effectively an 18th-level character, not a 10th-level one.
