So, other players: what to

So, other players: what to do about the orb.  We should probably confer before striking any deals on the spot.  Zhegre would suggest that it's obviously a dangerously powerful artifact that predates probably any civilization we have record of around here, kobold or dwarven, and that it may be a bad idea for any group to actually claim ownership of it.  Such things have proved a corrupting temptation so many times.  Also, he'd want to issue the caveat that we would like to see the results of any explorations as to the thing's utility -- legend lore's good for this kind of thing, but it takes a long time to cast -- and we may need sudden access to the thing if their mission turns in that direction.  Otherwhise, it would likely be quite inconvenient to be hauling an artifact around in the wilderness randomly, and so keeping it under lock and key in a settled area is probably for the best.  Some of this could probably be conviently discussed with the High One with the rest of our business.  What are you guys' takes on it?

As for the rest of the stuff, he is feeling quite open-minded and group-oriented with regards to the other treasure, having already been provided with divine lewtz this trip.  ^_^  The gems are probably safe, but a quick examination of the cloak and the torch seems called for.  The cloak is likely okay, but I bet that Torch has bad vibes attached, and we may want to see to it that it's disenchanted.
