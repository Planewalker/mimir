"No you've no love for my kind, child of Gith," Lavender sneers, "and you'll not let me live will you? Still, I have much to barter, and so little to ask in return. You githyanki have discovered who was in charge of this little opperation, and you're probably all proud of yourselves, yes? But did you know I have portals to other rooms in Illithid House? It's true. You can go and slay others of my kind if you wish. I'll even show you where the portals are and tell you their keys, how's that? Surely worth a few more moments of life? There is also so much treasure here for the taking, you need only reach out your hands and take it." Lavendar holds up the key ring it used to unlock the vault. There are several keys of various types on it. Ii feels the compulsion to loot the vaults forthwith.

"All I want in return is that you do not make my death quick. Hss hss hss." Lavendar chuckles, "I am a Factotum, you see, in the Society of Sensation. Most of my associates are Sensate namers.
