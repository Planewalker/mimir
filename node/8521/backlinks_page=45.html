[Got a bit of action to

[Got a bit of action to catch up on]

The Astral skiff circles the garden courtyard after 'dropping off' Na'rai.  Off on the opposite side of the gazebo, it drops a number of explosives and incendiaries, creating a brownish smoke of alchemist fire and plant matter.  It disappears from sight, but quickly comes back, aiming for the gazebo and moving at ramming speed.  There is a loud crash, and the gith see the roof of the gazebo shudder and tilt, but it does not fall.

In the air, Ar'dru and Kirath can see more.  The skiff managed to crash right through one pillar, and sever a second.  The second crash took its momentum away, however, and when the top of the second pillar comes down on it, the skiff is very nearly driven to the ground.  It slowly moves forward, turning and trying to get away again.  All six teams of soldiers had been firing arrows at the skiff on approach, which proved completely ineffective, so now that it is reachable, two teams swarm it.  The shieldmen set up in front of it, and the spearmen rush in and try to get the bottom hatch open.  The shieldmen and bowmen do manage to stop the skiff from moving, though it takes all of their efforts.  Ther efforts are in vain when Krath drops a fireball on them.  All six slaves run around screaming with their tunics aflame, falling to the ground dead in short order.  The armored and tougher slave-soldiers survive, but the skiff is now free to escape, and rises into the air again.

Ar'dru throws a black smoker as he swoops over the gazebo with Kirath, then lands in the courtyard, throwing a burning dust at his own feet.  The smell is acrid, and even Ar'dru's flame-resistant lungs balk at first to breathing the stuff in.  He lands between two groups of guards, and thinking they have the half-dragon flanked, they move in.  They do not realize their mistake at first.  To Ar'dru, the expanding cloud looks like a white smoke with little flaming sparks crackling throughout, but from a distance it looks like a kicked-up cloud of dust or white ash.  The squads move in, and the spearmen are close enough to strike at Ar'dru before they get a real whiff of the burning dust cloud.  The cloud expands rapidly, however, and the yuan-ti and their slaves soon find themselves in a cloud of something they cannot breathe.  They try to retreat, but the shieldmen, closer, weaker, and less mobile, are unable to escape the cloud and are overwhelmed, coughing until blood runs from their mouths.

None of the spears were able to pierce Ar'dru's armor, and now the spearmen try to escape the expanding cloud in a panic.  [Ar'dru gets an AoO]
