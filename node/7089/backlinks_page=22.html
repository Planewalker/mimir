"I...", Sheeram begins to answer to the Masked Gnoll. She stops when Ohalian begins to speak, then walks over to him.

"You think THIS is easily fixed?"

Abruptly, she throws back her head, the muscles throughout her body straining. As she screams out loud, the hall is filled with darkness, and a strong wind comes up - suddenly throwing open the front doors, hitting Ian and throwing him on the ground.

Sheeram's skin turns ash dark, and you can see her finger nails grow while a cloud of darkness forms around her body. From a distance, you can hear screams of pain and terror... until you realize they come out of her mouth, along with her own scream. Those screams become louder and louder, until the hall is filled with a Pandemonic cacophony of pain, and small whirlstorms shake the entire mansion like an earthquake.

Maybe ten seconds later - although it seemed like minutes or hours - everything calms down again, the light returning, the wind ceasing and the screams stopping.

Sheeram breathes in and out a few times, then straightens again.

Now, she is no more the beautiful young girl you have seen before. Her face is hard, distorted, and two large fangs reach out of her mouth. Her eyes are like black clouds, her skin appears dark and dead.

"You really think this is easily fixed?" she asks with a rasping, dark voice. "I am half dead inside, and I feel the deaths of all those both I and my hated vampire lord have killed. They are within me, screaming, dying, and I die with them every moment of my existence. Tell me how to fix that."
