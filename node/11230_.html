You could make her a shadowcaster (Tome of Magic) with druid as her base class and some levels of mystic theurge.

You could also just make her a wizard/druid/mystic theurge so that she isn't restricted in her spellcasting at all. That'd probably be truest to Chris Avellone's intentions.

She should be in her epic levels so that she can cast true dweomers. Ravel can do things, like sever a man's mortality, that no one else can do.

If you kill her you're only severing one branch. She still exists, her branches winding throughout the planes.
