Interesting Bartenders

A zern (Monster Manual IV) that derives alcohol from a variety of biologically engineered aberrations, including an arcanavore that distills alcohol from the magic it consumes. Incantifers are especially fond of this drink.

A kython (from the Book of Vile Darkness, not a kyton/chain devil) that uses a modified acid spitter and its own mucous sacs to create drinks, mostly for a fiendish and tiefling crowd.

A sibriex (Fiendish Codex I) that uses its black bile and demon-grafted cows to create potent fiendish milk-based drinks. This is sounding like a theme; maybe it should share the same bar as the kython.

Come to think of it, one of the illithids of Zoon from the Monster Manual V had a thing that made monsters from quintessence; it could be a bar tender too. I don't have that book yet, so no details for you.

What else? A Signer psion who makes drinks with her mind (as Astral constructs), a Believer of the Source who makes drinks from raw ether, an Athar who serves drinks in desecrated holy icons (here you go, one gin and tonic in the blaspheme-covered head of an icon of the blessed virgin Coatlicue), a Hardhead who will sell you drinks only between 8 hours after peak and antipeak, and not on any day that any religion he's aware of considers a day of rest, a Xaositect who will serve you shoes, declaring loudly that they are delicious eggs, a mind flayer who serves spinal fluid and Brain Daiquiri, a warden archon who serves the distilled joy of petitioners, an eladrin who serves alcohol that can make mortals fall into vivid dream-sleeps that last years or centuries or coffee that can make them stay awake for equally long, an Anarchist that puts subversive messages in the bottom of her beer mugs, a Sinker whose bar is slowly falling apart, who describes in detail the damage the alcohol is doing to your liver, and a Cipher who can serve drinks faster than you can blink.

Oh, how about a water elemental that lives in kegs of alcohol, and serves you parts of itself?

Or a golem that's an animate beer tap.

A ravid that infuses every drink with positive energy, and brings mugs and pint glasses to life to bring your drink to you.

A bloated ormyrr with ooze mephit servers, The ormyrr is deeply in bed with the criminal underworld, and pays protection money to Shemeshka the Marauder and reports to her on all activities she might find of interest.
