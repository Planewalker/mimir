Bladedancer wrote:
Do we know anything at all about the mismatched githyanki that are willing to join the black fleet?
Captain Styrnn left with his most loyal githwarriors, leaving behind mostly random gith, survivors whose squads are gone (kind of like Azthri). To cut the suspense, I'll tell you my idea was this: Since Ib'm already offered to take in any random githwarriors, basically any githyanki who wanted to follow you would decide so, a number who did not would instead stay in Starmantle. The way the PCs 'took command' would determine about how much of each. That is, unless the PCs wanted to be more strict in their pressment, but like I said before, Pale-Mist tried the forceful recruitment of the PCs, and got a revolt.

The graith are a different matter, however. The barbarian pirates of the Black Fleet are just that. They are happy to let the githwarriors act as boarders; they are effective and willing to take on the danger, and the gith care little for monetary wealth (they tend to keep weapons and practical items instead). There is little doubt that there will be a mutiny or two somewhere along the line. Best advice: take the fastest / most easily manned surface ships and any flying Astral ships, and have a smaller but effective force that is still able to evacuate other githyanki. There should be enough githyanki willing to follow you that you can kick all the graith off a couple of the best sailing ships.
