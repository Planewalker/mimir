Well, I must offer an apology. I intended to try and revive the campaign. BoG'r is still, by far, the longest-running game on these boards. I re-read (almost) the entire thing. I still have not yet come up with a post to revive the game, leaving it open for anyone to post, while advancing the plot without railroading (a charge I am sensitive to, having had it leveled on me here in the past).

The task is not simple, however, and I'm not sure yet how to do it. Also, this game has always been a group effort. Not one of the original players was still active at the last leave-off. The only constants have been the DM, and the vision. There is nothing wrong with players interacting with each other, IC, either. Kirath, Ar'dru and Athri' are probably standing next to each other. They are also with the other Git'ribani NPCs, including Du'minh.

I am, humbly, deeply, sorry to those who have been frustrated by my own lack of progress at this point. I'm not sure right now if I can, or even want to, make any more promises to re-start things. I don't mind criticism if it's constructive, or if I can construct something out of it. Even so, I prefer aid to criticism.
