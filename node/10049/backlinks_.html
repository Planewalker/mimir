Thanks! Anyhow, I've

Thanks!

Anyhow, I've borrowed my friends MM2, so I'll  give it a quick run through with everything that applies to PS.

Barghests: two types of them make their return in MM2, one which a bugbear (a 4th level brute) and the other as a hobgoblin (a 7th level controller). Both look interesting, the bugbear steals attacks, while the hob steals HP.

Bebilith: Doesn't destroy your armor anymore, but gives a cumulative penalty each time you get hit. Level 18 Solo Brute.

Dretch: A 2nd level Brute and really close to what I did with the Mane, so I think they tried to role them together. Using the Manes as minions should keep them distinct though.

Nycaloth ("Nycademon"): Annoying name aside, what they did with the Nycaloth is really interesting. They've give them the ability to Snatch enemies up into the air, either to separate them, or (more likely) to drop them and hit them with the ground. 

Yochol: Loth's favorite handmaidens. Next.

Erinyes: There's something called the Erinyes in here, but it's a warrior and "arbiter of justice for devils" that in no way resembles the original Erinyes except for being female. I personally will continue using the Sucubus until something ends up being homebrewed.

Kyton: A greater version now exists in the Gorechain Devil.

Djinn: They're back to rule the plane of air

Coure: Again, as a type of warrior in the Eladrin race called the Coure of Mischief and Strife 

Genasi: A whole bunch of them to be used as foes/NPCs

Marut: Three new versions, cause you can never have enough Maruts

Retrievers: They're backkkk.......  Eye rays and all, with the ability to planeshift.Level 27 Solider.

Slaadi: Two completely new sladdi, the flux sladd, which brings them into the heroic tier, albeit at level 9, and the slaad spawn, which is a mini slaadi minion. Complete with a template for the sladd spawner, who create slaad spawn through budding.

Phase Spider: Aka one more reprint and one more reason why I'm not buying Dragon or DDI.

I think that's all from the PS books, although there are a bunch of there classic monsters in there too. All in all, looks to be pretty good.

Several new 4E books have come out that have monsters in them. Here are some updates, and I'll add more as I find them.

Dungeon #186, "Creature Incarnations: Modrons" gave us several 4E incarnations of modrons.

Monster Manual 3 (MM3) contains the following:


  • Imix, Archomental of Fire

  • Ogremoch, Archomental of Earth

The Monster Vault (MV) contains the most up-to-date versions of the following:


  • Babau - Greater Tanar'ri

  • Balor - True Tanar'ri

  • Hezrou - True Tanar'ri

  • Marilith - True Tanar'ri

  • Vrock - True Tanar'ri

MV also added the following:


  • Dretch - Least Tanar'ri (L12 variant)

The Plane Below (TPB) has the following:


  • Blue Slaad

  • Black Slaad

  • Gray Slaad

  • Green Slaad

  • Red Slaad

  • White Slaad

The Dark Sun Creature Catalog (DSCC) added the following:


  • Psurlon (3 variants)

Dungeon Master's Guide 2 (DMG2) has the following:


  • Bar-lgura, Lesser Tanar'ri (Bloodcry Barlgura)

  • Mezzoloth, Lesser Yugoloth (Mezzodemon)

  • Sons of Mercy Basher and Wizard

  • Vrock, True Tanar'ri (Vrock Swordwing)

The Plane Above (TPA) has the following:


  • Githyanki (5 variants)
